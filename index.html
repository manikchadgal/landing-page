Indian Institute of Information Technology, Allahabad

The Indian Institute of Information Technology, Allahabad, is a public university located in Jhalwa, Prayagraj district, in Uttar Pradesh. It is one of the twenty-five Indian Institutes of Information Technology listed by the Ministry of Education, and is classified as an Institute of National Importance.

A very good coding culture with different coding clubs
A very good junior-senior culture and no ragging
Beautiful , clean & green campus
